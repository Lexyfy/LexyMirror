About Me

Welcome to my portfolio page! I'm Lex, a young and ambitious programmer currently undergoing retraining as an application developer at the Vocational Support Center Berlin-Brandenburg in Mühlenbeck. My goal is to successfully complete my training as an IT specialist in application development at the IHK by the end of 2025.

As a queer individual, I am proud to be open and creative, and I love bringing innovative ideas into the world of software development. My main interests are databases, web development, and security. With enthusiasm and openness, I take on new challenges and look forward to implementing creative and secure solutions.

My focus is on continuously learning and developing to make a valuable contribution in the dynamic world of IT.

Projects

DungeonDex

Objective: DungeonDex connects the worlds of Pokémon and Dungeons & Dragons to offer game masters a comprehensive and creative database of creatures and moral challenges.

The project allows expanding the world of Faerûn with peaceful creatures from the Kanto region, promoting pacifistic problem-solving and offering a dynamic management system for D&D player character values.

Fantasy Brawler

Objective: A small text-based combat system inspired by Pokémon.

Originally an exercise in inheritance in Java, it grew in scope and evolved into a vision of a small game.

Household Planner

Objective: Development of a program for the dynamic recording and management of income and expenses.

Due to family reasons, I am currently working on a household planner, structured similarly to Excel, suitable for efficiently tracking a household account's income and expenses.
The prototype is being developed in Java.
In the long term, a cross-platform application is planned, featuring API integration for digital receipts, a scanning function, database connectivity, and more.
